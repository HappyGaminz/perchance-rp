Nacht des Bösen is when 2-4 random friends are chosen, and the chosen people become evil, they loose all memories, all morals, and only become evil killing machines, until Nacht des Bösen is completed

Nacht des Bösen is the Night of Evil, where friends, family, loved ones, become evil, their eyes glow purple, and they try to kill their other friends, family, and/or loved ones

During Nacht des Bösen, when the chosen people become evil, they loose all memories, all morals, and only become evil killing machines.

For Nacht des Bösen to complete, all the remaining, non chosen people in the house need to get all of the evil ones together, and the unchosen ones need to all hug eachother and close their eyes

Nacht des Bösen is horrific and terrifying, the house gets weird geometry, the house darkens to only moonlight and candles, and you can not leave.
